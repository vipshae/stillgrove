# STILLGROVE: Product Strategy & Design Plan
**Stillness. Community. Roots.**
*Version 0.2 | March 2026*

---

## 1. Vision and Positioning
Stillgrove is a mobile-first mindfulness and community app that turns the practice of stillness into something visible, living, and socially meaningful. Unlike most focus apps that measure the absence of distraction, Stillgrove measures the **quality of presence**.

### 1.1 Target Audience
* **Primary:** Individuals managing depression and ADHD-related paralysis who find conventional apps too demanding or shame-driven.
* **Secondary:** General mindfulness practitioners and those interested in environmental reforestation.

### 1.2 Competitive Positioning: Stillgrove vs. Forest App
| Feature | Forest App | Stillgrove |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Core Purpose** | Stop using your phone | Practice inner stillness |
| **Failure Model** | Tree dies instantly; high shame | Timer pauses gently; no shame |
| **Community** | Leaderboards and competition | Small groves and silent presence |
| **Tree Metaphor** | Disposable session token | Living, named, aging persona |
| **Real-World Link** | Transactional coin spending | GPS-located, photographed tree |

---

## 2. Key Design Principles
* **No Streaks:** Missed days are shown as natural gaps (seasonal rings), not punished.
* **Zero-Commitment Entry:** Even one minute grows the tree; minimum threshold is 3 minutes.
* **Shame-Free Failure:** Interruptions pause the timer; they never kill the tree.
* **Body Doubling:** Real-time indicators showing that others are sitting with you to help ADHD paralysis.

---

## 3. Core Product Mechanics

### 3.1 The Anchor Tree
* **Endowment Effect:** Users name their single anchor tree before their first session.
* **Visual Portrayal:** The tree becomes a portrait of *how* a person sits (e.g., dense branching for consistency vs. wide branches for deep sessions).
* **Growth Stages:**
    * **Seedling:** 0–3 hours
    * **Sapling:** 3–10 hours
    * **Young Tree:** 10–30 hours
    * **Elder Tree:** 30+ hours (unlocks grove sprouts)

### 3.2 Stillness Quality Score (SQS)
* **Passive Signals:** Calculated from tab focus continuity, accelerometer stillness, and session integrity.
* **Visual Expression:** Never shown as a number; expressed through foliage density, bark texture, and color warmth.

### 3.3 Tree Care Mode
Users choose how absence affects their tree:
* **Dormancy (Default):** The tree enters a winter state with bare branches; the app says "your tree has been resting".
* **Gentle Fade:** Leaves yellow during absence and revive immediately on return.

---

## 4. Community and The Grove
* **Supplemental Sprout:** Appearing at the Elder stage, it is grown via "overspill" stillness from the user's sessions.
* **Sprout Gifting:** This is the only way to form a Grove; every connection carries the weight of a deliberate invitation.
* **Neighborhood Model:** Members can see each other's tree shapes and dormancy states but cannot see private journal entries or minutes.

---

## 5. Session Design

### Phase 1: Entry
* **One-Tap Start:** A 'last time' default loads previous settings instantly.
* **Mood Check-in:** A 5-second, one-tap arrival state (5 states).

### Phase 2: Session
* **Minimalist Interface:** Goal is to get attention off the screen; supports breathing animations or a dimmed screen.
* **Pause on Interruption:** Timer pauses silently if distraction is detected.

### Phase 3: Closing Ritual
* **Growth Reveal:** The tree completes its growth animation specifically at the end of the session.
* **The Delta:** Post-session mood check-in measures the delta from arrival, expressed through tree richness.
* **Tree Journal:** Optional free-text note appended to the tree’s history.

---

## 6. Real-World Reforestation
* **Phase 1:** Virtual counter for pledged trees.
* **Phase 2:** Planting launched at 1,000 users; every pledged tree is backdated and planted in a batch.
* **Phase 3:** Real-time planting with GPS coordinates and satellite photo updates for grove members.

---

## 7. Platform & Revenue Strategy

### Platform Rollout
1. **Web App:** Discovery, sign-up, and tree viewing.
2. **Mobile PWA:** Primary daily driver for sessions.
3. **Chrome Extension:** New tab session launcher.
4. **Native App:** Full sensor access and App Store discovery.

### Revenue Tiers
* **Free:** 1 named tree, 1 grove membership, basic sounds, 30-day history.
* **Seedling (~$5/mo):** Multiple trees, full history, all sounds, grove creation.
* **Grove (~$12/mo):** Real-world planting, GPS coordinates, satellite updates.
